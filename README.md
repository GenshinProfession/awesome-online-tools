---

## Why FluxCalc?

Most online tool websites have become bloated.

Pages are filled with ads.
Tools require accounts.
Calculations happen on remote servers.
Simple tasks turn into a frustrating experience.

FluxCalc was built with a different philosophy:

* ⚡ Instant results
* 🔒 Privacy-first by default
* 🖥️ Runs directly in your browser
* 🚫 No sign-up required
* 🎯 Focused on useful tools only

Every tool is designed to solve a real problem as quickly as possible.

## Core Principles

### Privacy First

Your data stays on your device.

Whenever possible, calculations are performed locally in your browser.

No unnecessary tracking.
No account required.
No personal information collected.

---

### Fast by Design

FluxCalc is built with modern web technologies focused on performance.

No heavy dashboards.
No complicated workflows.

Open the tool.
Enter your values.
Get the result.

---

### Built for Utility

We don't publish tools just to increase page count.

Every tool exists because someone actually needs it.

The goal is simple:

Create the fastest way to solve common problems.
